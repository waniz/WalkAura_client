What the current Gear screen has wrong:
  • Slot label colors (Head gold, Shoulder orange, Ring red…) look like quality tiers — but they're just decorative slot colors. Two color systems collide. Player can't tell at a glance which slots have epic vs. legendary items.
  • Empty slots look the same as filled slots (just no icon). No visual hint of what fits there.
  • "Average Gear Level: 0" is small grey text. It's the most important number on the screen — should be the hero.
  • ~40% of the screen is dead space below the doll. No stat preview, no set bonuses, no loadouts, no "what should I upgrade next" affordance.
  • No item comparison flow. Player must tap inventory → memorize stats → tap gear → tap slot — too many steps.
What changes in the redesign:
  • GEAR SCORE RIBBON — top ribbon with bold gold-bordered "GEAR SCORE 142" disc as hero, flanked by Slotted/Set Bonus left and Avg iLvl/Upgrades-in-bag right. Player sees power level immediately.
  • SLOT QUALITY = ITEM QUALITY — slot border color matches equipped item's quality (epic purple, legendary orange, mythic gold). Empty slot = dashed gray. Label below colored to match. Replaces the random slot-decoration colors. Now player sees their epics glow purple from across the room.
  • EMPTY-SLOT SILHOUETTES — empty slots show 18% opacity grayscale icon of expected item type (a faded helmet for Head, faded ring for Ring). Makes the doll feel "designed for me to fill" not "broken".
  • ITEM-LEVEL PIPS — each filled slot has a small gold corner pip showing iLvl ("22"). Fast scan: which slot is dragging your average down?
  • UPGRADE INDICATORS — green ↑ badge on slots where a better item exists in your inventory. The "Upgrades: ↑ 3 in bag" ribbon stat tells you the count.
  • GEMMED MARK — small ✦ in corner for gemmed/socketed items.
  • STAT PREVIEW — 6-stat grid showing what your gear contributes to derived stats. When you tap an inventory item, deltas show inline (green +3, red -2). Eliminates the "what does this even do" gap.
  • COMPARE OVERLAY (right phone) — tap an inventory item while in Gear and a comparison card slides up: side-by-side equipped vs candidate, with delta pills (+22 Phys DEF, +14 Score) and a one-tap EQUIP button colored to candidate's quality. Score preview also updates in the top ribbon (142 → 156).
  • EMPTY-SLOT TAP — tapping an empty slot opens inventory pre-filtered to that slot type. No more scrolling through 27 items hunting for a chestpiece.
