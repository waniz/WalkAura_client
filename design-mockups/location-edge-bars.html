Edge bars — what changed:
  • CLOSED = RAILS — two gold vertical-text rails dock the right edge (ACTIVITIES top, PEOPLE bottom). Icon + count disc + ‹ pull-chevron. Activities stub turns green when an activity is active; People stub pulses when a fresh offer exists.
  • OPEN = DRAWER OVER HERO — tap a rail and the sheet slides left over the painted hero (220ms) with a 55% scrim. Tap scrim, ✕, or the rail again to close.
  • BOTH INDEPENDENT — each opens on its own; rails stay docked so either is always reachable. Open both: most-recent sits on top, the rear peeks 8px on its left. Tap the rear rail to bring it forward.
  • ACTIVITIES DRAWER — the existing 3-col XP-ring picker, lifted verbatim out of the inline scroll.
  • PEOPLE = CANONICAL NPC HOME — full NPC Offer Frames (face circle, offers, gold Accept). The Quest screen's Available tab defers here later.
  • HUB RECLAIMS THE PAINTING — main column now carries only live state (quest tracker + active-activity card); the picker left the scroll, so the hero grew to 300px.
